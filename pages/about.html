Basirhat Mobisell Owner

At Basirhat Mobisell, we are not just a secondhand smartphone company; we are a community-driven venture founded on the principles of sustainability, affordability, and reliability. Behind our mission stands the innovative and dedicated mind of Biswajit Dutta, the driving force that brought this vision to life. Biswajit Dutta is not just a business owner; they are a tech enthusiast with a passion for environmental conservation and technology accessibility. With years of experience in the tech industry and a keen eye for market trends, Basirhat Mobisell embarked on a journey to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and responsible consumerism. Their commitment to reducing electronic waste and promoting eco-conscious practices led to the birth of BAsirhat Mobisell.

Join Us on the Journey

Biswajit Dutta invites you to join Basirhat Mobisell on this remarkable journey toward a greener, more connected future. By choosing our refurbished smartphones, you are not just getting a high-quality device; you are becoming a part of a movement that values both technological innovation and environmental preservation. Together, we can redefine the way the world views secondhand electronics and create a more sustainable tomorrow.

Thank you for being a vital part of our story.
